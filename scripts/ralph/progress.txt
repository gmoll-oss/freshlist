## Codebase Patterns
- expo-notifications v55 requires `shouldShowBanner` and `shouldShowList` in `NotificationBehavior` (in addition to `shouldShowAlert`)
- Use `SchedulableTriggerInputTypes.DAILY` with `hour`/`minute` for daily local notifications
- Use `SchedulableTriggerInputTypes.WEEKLY` with `weekday`/`hour`/`minute` for weekly notifications (weekday 1 = Sunday)
- Services are organized in `src/services/supabase/` (DB) and `src/services/ai/` (Claude); new services go in `src/services/`
- Hooks go in `src/hooks/` and are integrated via `_layout.tsx` AuthGate component
- Cancel previous scheduled notifications by tag before rescheduling to avoid duplicates
- AI services use `callWithRetry<T>()` from `./utils` which parses JSON response; prompts must request JSON-only output
- Supabase upsert with `onConflict` requires a unique constraint on the conflict columns
- Use `getCurrentUserId()` from `./pantry` in all supabase services for auth
